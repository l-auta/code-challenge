Speed Detector

Our speed detector program aims at reducing the amount of accidents that happen everyday on our roads. Using our speed detector controls drivers to maintain a speed of 70km/h as for every 5km/h above that a demerit point is given. However for a speed below 70km/h the driver gets an approval message from the system.

!WARNING!

Going above our limit of 12 points will result in your license being suspended!
